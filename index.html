Kids Side Scroll Adventure (Prototype)

Slow gameplay, clear feedback, and easy controls for family iteration. Click game area once if keys stop responding.

Controls

Move: Left/Right arrows, Jump: Up arrow

Level 1: W (laser), E (shooting star)

Level 2: M (cannon), F (speed), X (strength), G (flight)

When level 1 ends, game auto-loads level 2.

Press R any time to restart current level.
